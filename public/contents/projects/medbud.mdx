> ## Short Explanation

Medbud is a service that offers sms based management and medical assistance to hospitals and patients.

> ## Project Goals

It is a service that aims to provide management assistance to hospitals not equipped with the latest technology and also to patients who are not tech savvy.
It is created using Remix,Tailwindcss,Typescript,Prisma and Postgresql. It also has a flutter app which acts as a server for sms messages. Allowing admin
staff to only use sms to manage the hospital.

> ## Features

### Hospital Management

- **Patient Management** - The hospital staff can add patients to the database and also update their details.
- **Doctor Management** - The hospital staff can add doctors to the database and also update their details.
- **Appointment Management** - The hospital staff can add appointments to the database and also update their details.

### Emergency Services

- **Emergency Services** - The hospital staff can add emergency services to the database and also update their details.
- **Emergency Contacts** - The hospital staff can add emergency contacts to the database and also update their details.

### Medical Assistance

- **Medical Assistance** - Medical Assistance can be provided remotely using Davinci III model.
